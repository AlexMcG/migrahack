About Chicago MigraHack

The purpose of Chicago Migrahack is to foster the use of technology and open data to innovate information around immigration. In Spanish, the word "migra" refers to immigration."

"We want to bring together our communities to create innovative platforms around one of the most debated topics today: Immigration

You can find MigraHack on Twitter and Facebook.

About the App

This app was built over the course of two days during the Chicago MigraHack Hackathon that took place May 31 - June 2, 2013 in Chicago, Illinois at the incubator and coworking space, Cibola. The data used in this app was obtained from www.data.gov and can be found in its raw form here.

It was created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It utilizes the Flatstrap CSS framework, jQuery, and the Highcharts JavaScript library; all data is being pulled into the app using a custom written parser also written in JavaScript.
